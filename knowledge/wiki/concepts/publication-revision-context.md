# Publication Revision Context

## Summary

The MBE revision response draft captures how PhyloMovies should be framed in
the manuscript and software release after reviewer feedback. Source:
`revision/MBE_revision_response_draft.md`.

## Key Claims

- Rogue-taxon visualization should be framed as complementary to quantitative
  methods, not as a replacement. Source:
  `revision/MBE_revision_response_draft.md`.
- FastTree should be framed as an exploratory bundled default rather than a
  publication-grade recommendation. Source:
  `revision/MBE_revision_response_draft.md`.
- Usability documentation should clarify zoom/pan, HUD behavior, MSA
  synchronization, and deployment limitations. Source:
  `revision/MBE_revision_response_draft.md`.
- Scalability claims should remain practical and avoid overstating performance
  for thousands of taxa. Source: `revision/MBE_revision_response_draft.md`.
